      ### Jude's World

      Yvris' latest project, _Jude's World_, is a solo, tarot-based
      journaling TTRPG in which a precocious preteen protagonist struggles
      to reunite their warring parents. The game both pays homage to and
      critiques "Parent Trap"-style narratives while revelling in a 90s
      sitcom aesthetic inspired by the likes of _Boy Meets World_, _The
      Fresh Prince of Bel-Air_, and _Clarissa Explains It All_.

      Players take on the role of the titular Jude: a 12-year-old who's
      desperate to grow up and certain that they'll do a better job of it
      than their parents, Mika and Jamie, who just announced they are
      getting a divorce. Steer your version of Jude through a complicated
      time in their young life and unleash your inner outraged
      almost-teenager. Just remember to tuck them neatly back into your
      subconscious before you go out in public.

      Jude's World employs a traditional Rider-Waite tarot deck to generate
      story prompts as well as a 2d6 system for resolving Jude's attempts
      to rekindle Mika and Jamie's romance. Play through important
      milestones on Jude's journey into teenhood and explore their family's
      past through the keepsakes and knickknacks you uncover around their
      home. Investigate what happened in Mika and Jamie's relationship. Is
      it salvageable or was it always doomed to fail? If there is hope of
      reuniting them, is Jude the right kid for the job? Either way,
      getting these two people back together won't be as simple as it is
      on TV.
