      Button Kin Games is a small independent TTRPG studio based in
      Manchester, UK. It was established in 2020 as an imprint for works by
      Yvris Burke, covering her adventure content for more traditional systems
      as well as her standalone roleplaying games.

      Having run her first game just before the dawn of the pandemic era, Yvris
      (pronounced _Eve-ris_) suddenly found herself with a lot of time to
      immerse herself in different systems and roleplaying experiences,
      developing a borderline obsession. No more than 6 months later she
      decided to start making her own games. Friends of Yvris will recognise
      this charming combination of hubris and masochism as quite typical.

      ## Yvris's Work

      [_Bumbling_](/releases/bumbling/), her reality TV TTRPG about sassy,
      cutthroat llamas [_Drama Llamas_](/releases/drama-llamas/), and her

      [_streaming_](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WNjlJOIvbAI) with
      [_Girls Run These Worlds_](https://www.twitch.tv/girlsruntheseworlds).
