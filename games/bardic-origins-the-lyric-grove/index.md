  - type: section-header
    intro: |
      Doubly blessed by Milil, god of bards, and Eldath the river spirit, the
      grove is protected on all sides by a near-impassable wall of tangling
      vines and thorns as tall as the tallest tree in this ancient wood.

      Within its enchanted borders grow the oldest and wisest of trees, their
      mingled canopies alive with the trilling of colourful songbirds sacred
      to Milil. Water trickles and pours through many small streams,
      collecting in a crystal-clear pool imbued with healing powers by
      Eldath.

      The Lyric Grove's inhabitants are plucked from the downcast of society
      by Queen Synleoth. If they're not yet bards when they come to her,
      their latent talents soon emerge. She's a saviour of wounded rangers,
      a mother to abandoned children, and a figure of worship to pilgrims who
      come for the blessings of her patron gods.

      **As part of our Bardic Origins series, this supplement includes a
      detailed setting, NPCs, and a one-shot adventure, as well as new items
      to add flavour to your game.**
